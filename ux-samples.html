Information architecture is the cornerstone of user experience. In addition to making things "look pretty" they also need to "work properly." It's of the utmost importance to pay attention to the style of interaction as well as the style of the er-- style. Usually, this is toward the beginning of the process and involves wireframing, functional diagrams and/or schematics to design the flow of information and interactive elements from the user's perspective.
